# How Laser Cutting Improves Accuracy in Metal Fabrication

Precision is everything in metal fabrication. A deviation of even a millimeter can mean the difference between a perfect fit and a costly rework. This is where CNC fiber laser cutting has transformed the industry.

## What Is Fiber Laser Cutting?

Fiber laser cutting uses a high-power laser beam focused through a fiber optic cable to cut metal sheets with extreme precision. The laser beam is controlled by a CNC system that follows programmed cutting paths derived directly from CAD files.

## Accuracy Advantages

Compared to traditional cutting methods:

- **Tolerances within ±0.1mm** — far tighter than plasma cutting or manual shearing
- **Minimal heat-affected zone** — less warping and material distortion
- **Clean edge quality** — minimal dross, reducing secondary finishing
- **Complex geometry** — intricate shapes impossible with mechanical cutting
- **Repeatability** — identical parts every time, batch after batch

## Applications in HVAC Manufacturing

For HVAC duct manufacturing, laser cutting enables:

- Precise fitting of duct sections and flanges
- Complex damper and louver components
- Custom brackets and supports
- Nesting optimization for minimal material waste
