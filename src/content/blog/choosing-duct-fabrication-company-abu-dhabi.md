# Choosing the Right Duct Fabrication Company in Abu Dhabi

Selecting the right duct fabrication partner is one of the most important procurement decisions for any HVAC or MEP contractor in Abu Dhabi. The quality, timing, and cost of your ductwork directly affect project delivery and building performance.

## Key Factors to Evaluate

### 1. Facility Location

A fabrication facility in or near Musaffah or ICAD means shorter delivery distances, lower transport costs, and easier site visits for quality inspections. Duct Masters operates from M-40, Musaffah — at the center of Abu Dhabi's industrial zone.

### 2. Manufacturing Equipment

Modern CNC equipment — laser cutters, press brakes, plasma cutters — ensures precision and consistency. Ask about the specific machinery a fabricator uses and whether it matches your project's complexity requirements.

### 3. Quality Standards

Does the fabricator follow SMACNA standards? Do they have quality control processes in place? Request information about their inspection procedures and whether they provide QC documentation.

### 4. Material Capability

Can they work with the materials your project requires — galvanized iron, stainless steel, aluminum? What gauge range can they handle?

### 5. Capacity and Lead Times

Can they meet your project schedule? What is their typical lead time for standard ductwork versus custom fabrications?

### 6. Project Experience

Have they worked on projects similar to yours — commercial buildings, industrial facilities, hospitals, hotels?

## Questions to Ask Potential Fabricators

- What is your typical lead time for a standard commercial ductwork order?
- Can you provide shop drawings for approval before fabrication?
- Do you offer delivery to project sites?
- What quality control documentation do you provide?
