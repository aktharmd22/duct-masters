# Duct Supply and Installation for Commercial and Industrial Buildings

Efficient duct supply and delivery logistics are critical for keeping HVAC projects on schedule. For commercial and industrial buildings in the UAE, coordinating duct manufacturing with site installation requires careful planning.

## The Supply Chain Challenge

HVAC ductwork is bulky and requires significant storage space. For large commercial projects, duct sections may need to be delivered in phases to match the installation schedule. Key considerations include:

- **Production scheduling** — manufacturing must align with installation phases
- **Transport logistics** — flatbed trucks for large duct sections
- **Site storage** — limited space on active construction sites
- **Handling** — proper lifting and positioning equipment

## Phased Delivery Approach

For medium to large projects, a phased delivery approach works best:

1. **Phase 1**: Main riser ducts and large trunk sections
2. **Phase 2**: Branch ducts and distribution sections
3. **Phase 3**: Terminal connections, grilles, and accessories
